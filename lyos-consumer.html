Common questions

Things people want to know.

What exactly is neonatal platelet secretome?
It's the growth factor-rich signaling fluid released by neonatal platelets from cord blood. Unlike adult PRP which draws from your own blood, LYOS-001 uses cord-derived material collected at birth — when regenerative signaling is at its highest. The result is a standardized, freeze-dried product with consistent potency every vial.
Is this the same thing as PRP?
No. Traditional PRP uses your own adult blood — already depleted of growth signals. LYOS uses standardized neonatal cord blood with significantly higher concentrations of growth factors. It's also freeze-dried, so every dose is identical regardless of which clinic or which day you visit.
Will my body reject it? Is it safe?
LYOS-001 is cell-free — it contains signaling proteins only, not live cells. This eliminates immune rejection risk. The product is investigational and not yet FDA-approved; clinical trials planned for 2027–2029 will formally establish safety and efficacy.
When can I actually get this?
LYOS-001 is not yet FDA-approved. Clinical trials are expected to begin in 2027, with proof-of-concept readout targeted for mid-2029. Join the interest list to be notified when trials open near you or when LYOS becomes commercially available.
